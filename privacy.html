Privacy Policy for CoopCycle Tools

Effective Date: 06 February 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to CoopCycle Tools! This privacy policy is designed to inform you about the collection, usage, and protection of your data when using our Chrome addon. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to safeguarding your information.

2. Information Collected

  • Login Token: CoopCycle Tools fetches a login token from app.sling.com for the purpose of authenticating with the Sling API and generating CSV files.

3. Data Transfer

  • Data Forwarding: The login token obtained from app.sling.com is securely forwarded to coopcycle.antoinebagnaud.me. This data transfer is essential for authenticating with the Sling API and generating CSV files.

4. Data Security

  • Encryption: We prioritize your data security. All data, including the login token, is transferred using encryption protocols to ensure it remains confidential during transmission.

5. Data Storage

  • No Data Storage: We do not store any data collected from your usage of CoopCycle Tools. Your data is only temporarily processed for the purpose of authentication and CSV generation and is not retained on our servers.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Third-Party Services: Please be aware that when using CoopCycle Tools, you may interact with third-party services like app.sling.com. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these services for further information about their data handling practices.

7. Consent

  • Consent: By using CoopCycle Tools, you consent to the collection and processing of your data as described in this privacy policy.

8. Updates to Privacy Policy

  • Policy Changes: We reserve the right to update this privacy policy as necessary to reflect changes in our data handling practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be reflected with a revised effective date.

9. Contact Us

  • Contact Information: If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy or the data we collect, please contact us at antoine.bagnaud@coopcycle.org
